About Us

Clothes for Everyone is a non-profit organization that's job is to collect used or new clothes and to give them to people in need. Always trying to give clothes to all the people who desperately need them in a not so good situation. The location of our headquarters is in Minneapolis, Minnesota so that we can collct all types of clothing including winter gear for those in need help in the winter months.
